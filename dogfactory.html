Welcome to the Dog Factory.

Here's how it works.

  1. First, follow this link.
  2. Enter the breed of dog you want into the prompt and click "Run."
  3. After a few moments, you'll have nine puppers to choose from. Click on your favorite one to enlarge it and then click "Screenshot." Hot Tip: Be sure to save the picture somewhere easy to find.
  4. Attach the photo to an email. Here's a helpful guide if you don't know how.
  5. In the body of the email please tell me your dog's name and one (1) of their favorite activities. Hot Tip: Activities include but are not limited to: Wagging, fetch, stealing food, prancing, drooling etc.

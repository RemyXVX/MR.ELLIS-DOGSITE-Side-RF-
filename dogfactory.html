Welcome to the Dog Factory.

Here's how it works.

  1. First, follow this link.
  2. Enter the breed of dog you want into the prompt and click "Run."
  3. After a few moments, you'll have nine puppers to choose from. Click on your favorite one to enlarge it and then click "Screenshot." Hot Tip: Be sure to save the picture somewhere easy to find.
  4. Attach the photo to an email. Here's a helpful guide if you don't know how.
  5. In the subjuct line please enter "(your name)'s Creature."
  6. In the body of the email please tell me your dog's name, their breed, and one (1) of their favorite activities. Hot Tip: Activities include but are not limited to: wagging, fetch, stealing food, prancing, drooling etc.
  7. Send the email to bullhorn@gmail.com

What now?

I will upload all submissions to our very fun Virtual Dog Kennel! Check back often and watch our kennel grow. Note: Please limit submissions to one (1) dog per person. Choose your dog wisely.
